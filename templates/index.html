How this tool works

This tool provides timing details and cost calculation for a car in a particular track.

Step - 1

Put a device with pen drive connected to it in a car for reading real-time data.

Step - 2

As a car moves in different tracks, the device will collect car data and save it in pen drive.

Step - 3

When a car signs-out, collect device and pen drive.

Step - 4

Open this tool and select a CSV file from pendrive to import data.

Step - 5

This tool will provide total time with cost calculation as per OEM and different tracks.

Step - 6

You can see the car path, total cost, timing details and a map on screen.
